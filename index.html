Aloha & Welcome!

I'm a Product Experience strategist with over 15 years of experience designing, building, and deploying software platforms across industries including cybersecurity, pediatric trauma resuscitation, libraries, and small businesses.

My philosophy is to design streamlined tools and seamless experiences using participatory methods that solve complex problems impacting our society. I love spending my time designing experiences that transform people’s lives so they can live deliberately and do more good in the world.

On this website, you can find more information about my current role, past experience, and the research I have contributed.
