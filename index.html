Aloha & Welcome!

I am a user experience researcher, designer, and strategist with a passion for participatory methods and finding the joy of design in the details. My roots are in Kailua-Kona, Hawaii, and I live and work in NYC. I am currently the UX Research & Design Manager at White Ops, a cybersecurity company specializing in the detection and prevention of sophisticated digital fraud.

On this website, you can find more information about my current role, past experience, and the research I have contributed. Please feel free to contact me if you want to get in touch.
